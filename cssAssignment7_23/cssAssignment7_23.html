Gentoo Penguin :-)

The Gentoo Penguin is the fastest penguin in the world. It was first discovered in 1781.

The origin of the name "Gentoo" is unknown, as the source of the name could have come from multiple places. They are the third largest penguin and have an operating system named after them called Gentoo. It's my favorite animal because there's an OS named after it, and penguins in general are heavily associated with Linux.
